About Us

Aperture Science, Inc. is a leading research and development company that specializes in innovative technological solutions. Founded in 1947 by Cave Johnson, the company has been at the forefront of cutting-edge science for over seven decades.

At Aperture Science, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and our relentless pursuit of scientific breakthroughs. Our team of world-class scientists, engineers, and researchers work tirelessly to develop new technologies and products that improve people's lives and change the world for the better.

Our diverse portfolio of products includes everything from advanced robotics and artificial intelligence to cutting-edge medical devices and renewable energy solutions. We are constantly exploring new fields and pushing the boundaries of what is possible, always with an eye towards making the world a better place.

In addition to our cutting-edge research and development work, Aperture Science also has a strong commitment to social responsibility and ethical business practices. We believe that business can and should be a force for good in the world, and we work hard to ensure that our products and practices reflect this belief.

If you're looking for a company that is committed to pushing the boundaries of science and technology, while also making a positive difference in the world, look no further than Aperture Science, Inc.

Projects

At Aperture Science, we are committed to developing innovative technological solutions that improve people's lives and make the world a better place. Our diverse product portfolio includes:

Advanced Robotics

Our advanced robotics division specializes in developing highly intelligent, adaptable robots that can perform a wide range of tasks. From industrial automation to personal assistance, our robots are designed to make life easier and more efficient for everyone.

GLaDOS

One of Aperture Science's most famous products is GLaDOS, the Genetic Lifeform and Disk Operating System. Originally developed as a central control unit for Aperture Science's testing facilities.

Handheld Portal Device

One of our most revolutionary products is the Handheld Portal Device, or "portal gun." This device allows users to create two connected portals on almost any surface, instantly transporting themselves or objects from one portal to the other

Long Fall Boot

The Long Fall Boot is a specialized piece of equipment developed by Aperture Science for use in our rigorous testing programs. These boots are designed to protect the user from high-velocity falls, allowing them to safely navigate through testing environments that would otherwise be too dangerous.
